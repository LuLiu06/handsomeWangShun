My hansome husband Wang Shun

Job title: Olympic Swimming Champion

To the world, he’s an Olympic champion, a record-breaking swimmer who’s made history for China. But to me, he’s so much more—my partner, my rock, and the man whose smile lights up my days beyond the pool.
My husband the athlete is extraordinary. But *my* Shun? He’s the one who holds my hand after the spotlight fades, and that’s the victory I cherish most.
